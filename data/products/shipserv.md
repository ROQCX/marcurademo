# ShipServ

## What is ShipServ?

ShipServ is a leading maritime e-procurement and supplier network platform that connects shipowners, operators, and managers with qualified suppliers and service providers. It streamlines the procurement process, improves supplier relationships, and helps companies achieve better pricing and service quality.

## Who Uses ShipServ?

- Shipowners and operators procuring supplies and services
- Fleet managers sourcing vendors for port calls
- Procurement teams managing supplier relationships
- Technical superintendents ordering spare parts and services
- Companies seeking competitive pricing and supplier diversity

## Key Workflows and Benefits

### Supplier Discovery and Sourcing
ShipServ provides access to a global network of verified maritime suppliers, enabling users to:
- Search for suppliers by location, service type, and capability
- Compare supplier ratings and reviews
- Access supplier profiles and certifications
- Discover new suppliers in emerging markets

### Request for Quotation (RFQ) Management
The platform streamlines the RFQ process with:
- Automated RFQ distribution to multiple suppliers
- Standardized quotation formats for easy comparison
- Real-time quotation tracking and updates
- Historical pricing data for benchmarking

### Purchase Order Management
ShipServ facilitates the entire procurement workflow:
- Convert quotations to purchase orders
- Track order status and delivery
- Manage supplier communications
- Generate procurement reports and analytics

### Supplier Relationship Management
The platform helps build stronger supplier relationships through:
- Performance ratings and feedback systems
- Supplier certification and compliance tracking
- Contract management tools
- Collaborative communication channels

### Benefits
- Access to a global network of verified suppliers
- Competitive pricing through transparent quotation processes
- Reduced procurement cycle times
- Better supplier quality and reliability
- Improved procurement visibility and control
- Cost savings through better pricing and reduced maverick spending

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Port Call Service Procurement
A vessel is scheduled for a port call in Singapore. The operations team uses ShipServ to send RFQs for bunkering, provisions, and crew change services to multiple verified suppliers. They receive competitive quotations within hours, compare options, and award contracts to the best suppliers.

### Example 2: Spare Parts Sourcing
A technical superintendent needs an urgent spare part for a vessel's engine. They search ShipServ's supplier network, find multiple suppliers with the part in stock, compare prices and delivery times, and place an order with the most suitable supplier.

### Example 3: Supplier Performance Tracking
A fleet operator uses ShipServ to track supplier performance across multiple port calls. They rate suppliers based on service quality, pricing, and reliability. This data helps them build preferred supplier lists and negotiate better terms with top-performing suppliers.
